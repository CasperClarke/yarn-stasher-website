Our Passion for Yarn

Do you have a large collection of yarn?

We know the challenge of keeping track of it all. Yarn Stasher helps you inventory and catalog your entire stash without typing any information. Simply take a picture of your yarn labels, and Yarn Stasher's AI automatically extracts all the details—brand, color, weight, fiber content, and yardage. Let technology do the work while you focus on what you love: creating beautiful projects.
